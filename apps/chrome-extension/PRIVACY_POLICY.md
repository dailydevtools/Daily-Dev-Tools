# Privacy Policy for DailyDevTools Chrome Extension

**Last Updated: February 8, 2026**

## Overview

DailyDevTools is a Chrome extension that provides developer utilities directly in your browser's side panel. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

This extension:
- ✅ Runs entirely locally on your device
- ✅ Does not transmit any data to external servers
- ✅ Does not track your browsing activity
- ✅ Does not require an account or login
- ✅ Does not use analytics or telemetry

## Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`) solely to:
- Remember your last-used tool preference

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not synced to any cloud service
- Can be cleared by removing the extension

## Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions required:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `sidePanel` | Display the tools interface in Chrome's side panel |
| `storage` | Save your tool preference locally |

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. All processing (JSON formatting, encoding, hashing, etc.) happens locally in your browser.
